Pizza

Pizza is a very delicious food that many people love, here is the best way to prepare pizza for all enthusiasts. This is a basic red sauce pizza that can be topped with any food that you may want.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Tomato Paste
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll dough and add sauce and toppings
  4. Bake
  5. Enjoy
